Hello there, and welcome to my homepage! My name is Md Ruhul Amin, and I am a PhD candidate at Department of Biological Science, University of Alberta. I am working in Dr. Declan Ali's group, studying the effect of cannabis on the development of zebrafish. My goal is to study the developmental effects of cannabinoid on developing organism. All I am trying to understand is what happens to the baby if an women takes marijuana during early pregnancy while it remains undetected. This will ultimately help us to developm a safer guideline of using marijuana during pregnancy. I maintain a list of my publications, previous research projects under the Research tab, and a list of other non-research projects under the Projects tab. You may also check out my Github profile.
